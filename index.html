What We Do

We are here for our fellow Americans. Founded by Charles Mardis, our institution focuses on helping our 15% of Americans with IBS.

What is IBS? Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) is a common disorder that affects the large intestine. Signs and symptoms include cramping, abdominal pain, bloating, gas, and diarrhea or constipation, or both. IBS is a chronic condition that you'll need to manage long term. IBS is a serious disease, Most dont take it seriously.
